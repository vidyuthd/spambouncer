SPAM Bouncer

The Future of SPAM Filtering

SPAM Bouncer uses Machine Learning and Keyword based filtering in order to filter out SPAM SMS. We collected thousands of real world messages to generate trained models using Machine Learning to fight SPAM SMS. Keyword based filtering allows user to configure specific words which can used to allow/filter SMS if it contains that word.

  • Security & Privacy : Filters SPAM SMS on device so that none of your texts ever leave your device. Your text messages are safe as they are not send to third party servers to decide whether its SPAM or not

  • Features : Preconfigured & changeable Keyword lists(Allow & Deny List) for keyword based filtering, SMS Reporting for reporting SPAM sms, register for DND to avoid Unsolicited Commercial Communication (UCC)/ Telemarketing Calls / SMS, iCloud Sync to save your preferences combined with accuracy of Machine learning to bring you hassle free experience in dealing with SMS SPAM.

  • Difference : We are on a mission to fight SPAM methodically and securely ensuring privacy is maintained all while being affordable. In doing so we tried to make this app as user friendly, intutive and informative as possible to educate user about ways of fighting SPAM. We are actively working in bringing more features to this app.
